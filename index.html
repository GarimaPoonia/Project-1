ABOUT US

Welcome to VR SunCycle & Smart Bike, where innovation meets fitness and fun! We are a passionate team dedicated to revolutionizing the exercise gaming industry through immersive experiences that inspire active lifestyles.

Our Mission

At VR SunCycle & Smart Bike, our mission is clear: to motivate individuals to engage in physical activity while enjoying the thrill of gaming. We believe that exercise should be enjoyable, and our project is designed to make fitness fun for everyone.

Overview

VR SunCycle & Smart Bike is a cutting-edge simulation game that combines virtual reality technology with real-world exercise equipment. Our project originated with the vision of creating an interactive gaming experience that seamlessly integrates with smart bikes, providing users with an unparalleled level of immersion.

Goals and Objectives

In this trimester, our primary objective is to enhance the existing VR SunCycle by introducing new missions and features. We aim to improve responsiveness and fluidity in bike turning controls while seamlessly integrating incline detection to adjust pedalling difficulty. Our focus also includes expanding the virtual world to provide a richer experience and facilitating easier testing of IoT devices and VR setups.

Key Innovations

A key innovation of our project is pedal-based motion, where real-life pedalling directly influences in-game movement. By introducing varying inclines, we add a realistic and challenging dimension to the exercise experience, making workouts more engaging and effective.

Trimester Deliverables

To achieve our goals, we have outlined a series of trimester deliverables, including developing cosmetic items for customisation, introducing specific objectives and missions, achieving seamless integration across platforms, creating tutorial levels for new users, establishing in-game shops, implementing point storage systems, and enhancing navigation features.

Long-term Vision

Looking ahead, our long-term vision involves fully integrating VR headsets into the project for an immersive experience, ensuring smooth interaction between IoT devices and the game, and introducing challenging elements like inclines and interactive objects to enhance realism and enjoyment.

Join us on this exciting journey as we redefine fitness gaming with VR SunCycle & Smart Bike. Get ready to pedal your way to adventure and fitness like never before!

GET STARTED

Ready to embark on your virtual cycling adventure? Follow these simple steps to get started:

  1. Set up your VR headset and smart bike according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  2. Launch the VR SunCycle & Smart Bike game on your VR device.
  3. Connect your smart bike to the game to synchronize your pedaling with in-game movement.
  4. Select your mission, adjust difficulty settings, and start pedaling!

Get ready to experience the ultimate fusion of fitness and fun!

Tips for an Enhanced Experience:

  • Adjust the seat and handlebars of your smart bike to ensure a comfortable riding position.
  • Choose a well-lit and open space for your VR setup to maximize safety and immersion.
  • Stay hydrated and take breaks as needed during your cycling sessions.
  • Explore different missions and terrains to keep your workouts varied and engaging.
  • Join multiplayer sessions to challenge friends or meet fellow cyclists from around the world.
